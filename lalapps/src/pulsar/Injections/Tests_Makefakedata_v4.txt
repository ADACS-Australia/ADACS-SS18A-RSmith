SUMMARY: The maximal relative errors seem large, but on inspection of
the SFTs, they seem to come from a few isolated "lines" in the
relative error, which are restricted to low-amplitude parts of the
spectrum. On inspection the two power-spectra look in excellent
agreement, and the mean-errors seem to better reflect this.

The (small) disagreement seen here is probably mostly due to
differences observed in the respective time-series, which seem to
originate from internal rounding/interpolation errors in LALSimulateCoherentGW().
